Welcome to Stackmesh:
Be the Internet

Welcome to project Stackmesh, where we're working to shift control back to YOU and protect our users' right to privacy. The project, in its most simplest form, is a distributed network/internet that's designed with privacy protection in mind, where each user can dictate what should and should not be accessible to 3rd parties. Currently the project is still in the early stages of development, but please check back soon for updates!
